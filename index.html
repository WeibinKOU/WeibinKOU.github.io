Biography

I am currently a PhD candidate at The Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE), The University of Hong Kong (HKU), supervised by Prof. Yik-Chung Wu. My research interest lies at the intersection of Federated Learning (FL), Large Models (LMs), Autonomous Driving (AD) and Robotic Perception.
